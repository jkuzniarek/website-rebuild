About

In case you've missed the sitewide self promotion I'm calling a banner my name is Julien Kuzniarek and I'm a Web Developer in Tampa, FL. I graduated from the University of South Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Information Science, which kickstarted my interest in programming and web development.

I built this site to serve as a personal digital billboard introducing myself and my work, as well as a blog of sorts. This site is dynamically served off pythonanywhere.com with a significant portion of the content stored in and retrieved from a SQL database. The primary desired externalities of converting my previous static website to this one were to simplify the publishing process for myself, to provide myself with knowledge and experience in web development using more than just HTML/CSS, to get hands on the Flask framework, and to demonstrate my familiarity with jQuery, SQL, and Python (my preferred language).

My personal interests include, organizational structures, cellular automata, artificial sentience, social design, urban planning and development, mass transit, and even history. These interests are each tied together and rooted in my love for systems analysis and design combined with my propensity to examine both the impact of the perspectives people hold on their actions and decisions, and the cascading effects that can occur from small changes to a system.
