About

In case you've missed the sitewide self promotion I'm calling a banner my name is Julien Kuzniarek. I'm currently an Integration Engineer at Net Synergy Virtual Solutions in Tampa, FL where I develop web scraping robots and data Extraction, Transformation, and Loading solutions. I typically work with a variety of tools such as RESTful interfaces, Kofax Kapow, Apache NiFi, and SQL databases.

I am a graduate of the University of South Florida (go Bulls!) where I earned a Bachelor of Science in Information Studies, and kindled my interest in programming and web development.

I built this site to serve as a personal digital billboard introducing myself and my work, as well as a blog of sorts. This site is dynamically served off pythonanywhere.com with a significant portion of the content stored in and retrieved from a database. The primary desired externalities of converting my previous static website to this one were to simplify the publishing process for myself, to provide myself with knowledge and experience in web development using more than just HTML/CSS, to demonstrate my familiarity with SQL and Python (my preferred language), and to get hands on with the Flask framework.

My personal interests include, organizational structures, cellular automata, artificial sentience, social design, urban planning and development, mass transit, and even history. These interests are each tied together and rooted in my love for systems analysis and design combined with my propensity to examine both the impact of the perspectives people hold on their actions and decisions, and the cascading effects that can occur from small changes to a system.
