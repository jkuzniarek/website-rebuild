About

In case you've missed the sitewide self promotion I'm calling a banner my name is Julien Kuzniarek. I'm currently an Integration Engineer at Net Synergy Virtual Solutions in Tampa, FL where I develop web scraping robots and data Extraction, Transformation, and Loading solutions. I typically work with a variety of tools such as Kofax Kapow, Apache NiFi, and SQL databases.

I am a graduate of the University of South Florida (go Bulls!) where I earned a Bachelor of Science in Information Studies.

My personal interests include, cellular automata, artificial sentience, social design, urban planning and development, mass transit, and even history. These interests are each tied together and rooted in my love for systems analysis and design combined with my propensity to examine the impact of the perspectives people hold on their actions and decisions.

I built this site to serve as a digital portfolio introducing myself and my work, as well as a platform for documenting my software creations and occasional thought experiments. This site follows the client server model with a significant portion of the content stored in a database. The primary desired externalities of converting my previous static website to this one are to simplify the publishing process for myself, to provide myself with knowledge and experience in web development using the Flask framework, and to demonstrate my capabilities in SQL.
